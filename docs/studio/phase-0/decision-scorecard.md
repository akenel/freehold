# Phase 0 · Workstream 4 — Decision scorecard

*The single place every finding lands, and where the go/no-go call gets made and
signed. If it isn't written here, it didn't happen. This is the document you hand the
boss at the end of Phase 0.*

---

## The decision, in one line

> **GO** to build the `CIS Companion` MVP **if ≥ 6 of 10** interviewed firms score
> **Pain ≥ 2 AND Existing Spend ≥ 2** — real pain *and* a budget already moving that a
> product could redirect. Otherwise, **rethink** using what we learned.

## How to present this to the boss

Don't hand him this whole file. Hand him:
1. **The one-line decision** at the top, with the **GO votes: X/10**.
2. **Three raw customer quotes** from §1.
3. **The gap memo** from §2 and the **certification verdict** from §3.

That's a five-minute brief that says: *we found the pull (or we didn't), we know the
competition, we know what it costs to be allowed to sell, and here's the call.* That is
what "business-grade, and I did my homework" looks like on one page.
