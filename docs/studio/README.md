# 🏗️ Studio — what to build, and why

This folder is the **strategy layer** for turning the Freehold starter kit into
sellable, business-grade software. It exists to answer one question a new lead has
to answer before writing a line of product code:

> *What do we build first, for whom, and how do we know people will pay for it?*

It is deliberately **paper before code**. The point is a direction we can defend to
the boss with sources, not a demo we can't back up.

## The decisions this folder is built on

| Decision | Choice | Where it's argued |
|---|---|---|
| Who the software is for | **Products to sell to other small businesses (SaaS)** | [00-brief](00-brief.md) |
| Market | **UK / Europe** | [00-brief](00-brief.md) |
| Monday deliverable | **A sharp strategy doc + roadmap** (no code changed yet) | this folder |
| Domain edge | **Construction, trades & field service first** | [01-pain-landscape](01-pain-landscape.md) |

## Read in this order

1. **[00-brief.md](00-brief.md)** — the situation, the mission, the constraints, and the honest risk of the path we picked.
2. **[01-pain-landscape.md](01-pain-landscape.md)** — the real, *sourced* pain points, segmented by business size and sector. The evidence.
3. **[02-app-portfolio.md](02-app-portfolio.md)** — candidate apps scored on opportunity vs. difficulty. What to build, what to skip.
4. **[03-roadmap.md](03-roadmap.md)** — the phased plan: what ships in 30 / 90 / 180 days.
5. **[04-team-and-freehold.md](04-team-and-freehold.md)** — how a junior team executes this on top of Freehold, and what Freehold has to grow to get there.

## How to use it with the boss

Don't hand him five files. Hand him **[00-brief.md](00-brief.md)** and the scorecard in
**[02-app-portfolio.md](02-app-portfolio.md)**. The rest is the working-out that
proves you didn't guess.
