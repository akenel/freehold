## How to present this to the boss

Don't hand him this whole file. Hand him:
1. **The one-line decision** at the top, with **GO votes X/10** and **Test 2 PASS/STOP**.
2. **Three raw customer quotes** from ¬ß1.
3. **The gap memo** (¬ß2) and the **accountant verdict** (¬ß3).

Five minutes that say: *we found the pull (or we didn't), we know the incumbents leave a
real gap (or they don't), we know who controls the sale, and here's the call.* That is
what "business-grade, and I did my homework" looks like on one page.
