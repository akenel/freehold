## What "Freehold" means

A **freehold** is land you own free and clear — no lease, no landlord, no ground rent. Nobody can raise your rent or evict you. That is this repo, for your software: **your code, your data, your infrastructure — yours, portable, and free.** The opposite of building your business on rented ground you can be priced off of tomorrow.

## What you get, out of the box

- 🔐 **Real login + roles** — Keycloak (OIDC) with RBAC, across **three environments: sandbox → staging → production**. Without this you're not going anywhere; it's here on line one.
- 🗄️ **Postgres** — your data, on a database that runs Fortune 500s.
- ⚡ **FastAPI (async Python)** + a **PWA front-end** — runs on any device with a browser, installs like an app, no app store, no three native versions.
- 📦 **One command up** — `docker compose up -d` and the whole thing runs.
- 🚀 **A deploy pipeline that won't break prod** — checkout → stamp the build → restart → health-check, with **encrypted, restore-verified backups that gate production.** The part nobody else ships.
- 🎫 **Feedback + QA built in** — an in-app feedback button, a backlog, a QA dashboard. Nothing falls through; every fix is remembered *with its reason.*
- ℹ️ **An honest status bar** — the live build number and SHA, so you always know exactly what's running.
